Lucent Modules

External intelligence that turns visibility into action. Discover risk, prioritize with GenAI context, and drive mitigation across your stack.

External Attack Surface Management (EASM)

Continuously discover and score external-facing assets to expose open ports, vulnerable services, and expired SSL. Track defined and auto-discovered assets. Identify access credentials tied to your estate and prioritize remediation with intelligence-driven scoring.

Compromised Credentials

Detect leaked emails and passwords across breaches, forums, markets, messaging apps, and info-stealer logs. Flag high-risk exposures for VIPs and critical roles. Stop account takeover before it begins.

Phishing Intelligence

Find impersonating domains, subdomain abuse, and suspicious SSL patterns to identify phishing kits early. Monitor permutations and lookalike domains at scale; accelerate takedown with infrastructure insights.

Vulnerability Intelligence

Monitor vulnerabilities tied to your stack—including trending CVEs and exploit trading. Prioritize patching based on exploitation chatter and sector relevance. See zero-day signals as actors market fresh exploits.

Credit Card Fraud Mitigation

Track underground trading of stolen cards linked to your institution. Use early alerts to cancel cards before monetization. Reduce losses and bolster customer trust.

Threat Actor Profiling

Generate dynamic profiles of actors across forums, social networks, and messaging channels. Review activity trends and extracted entities to anticipate TTPs and adjust defenses.

Explore (Ad‑Hoc Intelligence)

Run targeted queries for actors, domains, IPs, CVEs, exploits, and malware. Add results to your account data lake for correlation and enrichment. Answer new questions in minutes.

IOC Management

Centralize IOCs from commercial and proprietary feeds with rich context. Enrich with actor links, malware families, geographies, and confidence. Export via CSV or TAXII to downstream controls.
