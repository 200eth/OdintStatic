DRP Overview

DRP anticipates external risks to brand, people, and data across open and hidden channels. It combines persistent surveillance, early threat identification, and guided response. LUCENT operationalizes DRP with integrated CTI and EASM for end-to-end coverage. Reduce surprise and improve resilience.

DRP Implementation Framework

Phase 1: Collect signals across web, social, and underground sources tied to your brand and assets. Phase 2: Assess and classify threats by severity and impact. Phase 3: Communicate concise, actionable reports to decision-makers. Phase 4: Maintain continuous surveillance with iterative improvements.

Strategic DRP Applications

Monitor ransomware ecosystems and leak sites for early indicators and IOCs. Detect domain/IP spoofing, fake apps, and brand abuse for quick takedowns. Track credential harvesting, data auctions, and supply chain exposure. Protect executives and high-risk roles with targeted monitoring.

DRP with LUCENT
