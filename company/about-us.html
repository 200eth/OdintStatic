Our Story

Founded in 2018, Odint Technologies emerged from a vision to democratize enterprise-grade cybersecurity. We recognized that traditional security solutions were often too complex, expensive, or inaccessible for many organizations that desperately needed protection.

Our journey began with a small team of cybersecurity experts who believed that security should be proactive, intelligent, and accessible to all. Today, we serve thousands of organizations worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them navigate the complex landscape of digital threats.

Through our flagship product, Lucent, we've built a comprehensive cybersecurity platform that combines cutting-edge technology with practical usability, ensuring that organizations can focus on their core business while we handle their security needs.
