Insurance

Insurers face PII/PHI exposure, impersonation, and phishing. Lucent provides external intelligence, DRP, and integrations to accelerate fraud response and compliance.

Key Vulnerabilities

PII/PHI Exposure

Large volumes of PII/PHI attract data brokers and fraud rings.

Impersonation & Phishing

Brand impersonation and policyholder phishing drive claims fraud and ATO.

Compliance Pressure

Compliance pressure for proactive, documented risk management.

What LUCENT can do for you

  • Monitor dark web markets and leak sites for policyholder and claims data, with rapid severity scoring.
  • Identify phishing kits and fake agent sites; supply IOCs and takedown actions to protect customers.
  • Detect staff and partner credential exposure; enforce resets and watchlists for high risk roles.
  • Integrate feeds into fraud analytics and SIEM/SOAR to accelerate case investigation.
