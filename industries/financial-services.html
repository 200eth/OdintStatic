Financial Services

Banks, Islamic banks, and eWallets face fast-moving fraud, brand impersonation, and exposed attack surfaces. Lucent delivers external threat intelligence, EASM, and AI-driven triage to reduce loss and meet regulatory expectations.

Key Vulnerabilities

Underground Card Trafficking

Card and credential data appear on underground markets before legacy controls detect fraud.

Phishing & Brand Abuse

Phishing, look alike domains, and brand abuse drive account takeover and reputational loss.

Exposed Attack Surface

Exposed internet-facing systems and misconfigurations create high-value entry points.

Regulatory Pressure

Regulators expect proactive, audit ready external threat intelligence and faster incident reporting.

What LUCENT can do for you

  • Track credit card trafficking, breach dumps, and info stealer logs to trigger preemptive card blocks and forced resets.
  • Detect phishing kits and impersonating domains early; provide takedown guidance and exportable IOCs for blocking.
  • Use EASM to discover exposed assets, open ports, and expired SSL; prioritize by active exploitation chatter.
  • Apply GenAI scoring and MITRE ATT&CK mapping for rapid triage with executive/analyst dashboards and PDPA friendly reporting.
  • Integrate via STIX/TAXII and REST APIs into SIEM/SOAR to automate investigation, resets, and network blocks.
