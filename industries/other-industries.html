What LUCENT can do for you

  • Provide unified external threat intelligence across Surface, Deep, Dark Web, social platforms, and messaging channels with continuous collection.
  • Apply GenAI risk scoring, deduplication, and MITRE ATT&CK mapping to cut noise and prioritize by business impact.
  • Deliver DRP for brand and VIP protection: detect phishing kits, look alike domains, fake apps, and social media abuse with takedown guidance.
  • Run EASM to discover assets, exposed services, expired certificates, and misconfigurations, prioritized by external exploitation signals.
  • Detect compromised credentials and fraud indicators from breach dumps, stealer logs, and markets, with VIP watchlists and automated reset workflows.

  • Orchestrate early warnings and guided remediation using a recommendation library, task ownership, due dates, and closure tracking.
  • Integrate via STIX/TAXII, REST APIs, and CSV to SIEM, SOAR, EDR/XDR, WAF, and email gateways; send alerts to business stakeholders.
  • Offer executive and analyst dashboards with trend widgets and shareable, audit friendly reports aligned to PDPA expectations.
  • Enable rapid, cloud native onboarding with role based access and multi tenant operations for groups and MSSPs.
